Programming and Data for Policymakers

EVolve Boston is the work of four Harvard Kennedy School students as the culmination of a two-week class during January, 2020, “Programming and Data for Policymakers” with Dhrumil Mehta. None of us had web development or significant data visualization experience at the beginning of the course, and we learned a great deal through using Agile principles and Scrum to collaboratively work on GitHub and build this website with HTML, CSS, Javascript, and D3 data visualizations.
