A vision of carbon-free Boston

As part of its ambitious 2019 Climate Action Plan to reduce Greenhouse Gas emissions 50% by 2030 and 100% by 2050, the City of Boston is working on numerous levels to support a massive city-wide transition to electric vehicles.

EVolve Boston uses data to highlight the necessity of transitioning swiftly to zero-emissions vehicles. We also consider the greatest the obstacles to doing so, and how the City can best encourage this monumental transition of infrastructure and behavior.
