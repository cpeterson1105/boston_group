Future projections of EVs in Boston

In Boston, electric vehicles are projected to grow up to 50,000 if they follow the global projection.

In 2014, there are only 51 electric vehicles in Boston. However, according to the Global EV Outlook 2019, the number of EVs stock is going to constantly increase. In case of the EV30@30 Scenario that set the objective to reach a 30% sales share for EVs by 2030, the numbe will reach almost 300% of the stock of 2014. If Boston succeed to follow the expansion, the EVs in the city will exceed 15 thousand.
